* The 741 op-amp is used in 'comparator mode' with positive feedback.
* A capacitor connected to the inverting input charges and discharges through a resistor.
* The output switches between +Vsat and -Vsat of IC741.
* This repeated switching generates a square wave, while the capacitor voltage forms a triangular wave.
* The red LED blinks at one phase of output wave and white LED at opposite phase of output wave.
* The frequency of output wave can be altered by changing values of C and R.
